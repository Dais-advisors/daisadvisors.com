Know Your Obligation

Who Needs to File ITR?

ITR filing is mandatory for some and strongly recommended for others. Know where you stand before the due date.

⚠

Mandatory Filing

  • !Income exceeds basic exemption limit
  • !Company or Firm (any profit/loss)
  • !Bank deposits of ₹1 Crore or more
  • !Foreign travel spend above ₹2 Lakh
  • !Electricity bill exceeds ₹1 Lakh/year
  • !TDS / TCS of ₹25,000+ deducted
  • !NRI with income arising in India
  • !Foreign assets or foreign bank accounts
✓

Strongly Recommended

  • ✓Claim TDS refund from bank or employer
  • ✓Carry forward capital loss to next year
  • ✓Income below limit but TDS was deducted
  • ✓Applying for home loan or vehicle loan
  • ✓Applying for visa (USA/UK/Canada/Schengen)
  • ✓Freelancers receiving professional fees
  • ✓Students with FD interest or part-time income
  • ✓Maintaining a clean financial record
